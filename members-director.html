Introduction

My research centers on financial data science, business analytics, and data-driven decision making, with a particular focus on integrating data science and financial engineering to address real-world financial challenges. I strive to make financial services more intuitive and accessible by simplifying complex concepts and developing user-friendly tools for asset management.

Through this work, my goal is to contribute to both academia and industry by producing rigorous yet practical research outcomes that can be applied broadly in financial practice. With a strong academic background and a sincere passion for impactful research, I remain committed to advancing fintech innovation in ways that create meaningful and lasting value for society.
