Introduction

I am an Assistant Professor at Dongduk Women's University, specializing in Financial Data Science, Business Analytics, and Data-Driven Decision Making. My research integrates cutting-edge data science methodologies with financial engineering to address real-world challenges in finance and business.

My work encompasses three key areas:

1

I develop AI-driven solutions for quantitative finance, including portfolio optimization, algorithmic trading, and financial time-series forecasting.

2

I apply advanced analytics across business domains, leveraging time-series models, graph-based analytics, and NLP to extract actionable insights.

3

I design intelligent decision support systems that combine optimization techniques with user-friendly interfaces to solve complex business problems.

Through this interdisciplinary approach, I aim to bridge the gap between academic rigor and practical application, contributing innovations that advance both theoretical understanding and industry practice in financial data science.
