Introduction

My research centers on financial data science, business analytics, and data-driven decision making, with a particular focus on integrating data science and financial engineering to address real-world financial challenges.

Through this work, my goal is to contribute to both academia and industry by producing rigorous yet practical research outcomes that can be applied broadly in financial practice.
