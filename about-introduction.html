Introduction

동덕여자대학교 경영융합학부 금융데이터인텔리전스 연구실(FINDS Lab.)에 오신 것을 환영합니다.

오늘날 비즈니스와 금융 환경은 데이터 중심으로 급격히 변화하고 있습니다. 이제 경쟁력을 좌우하는 핵심 요소는 단순한 자본이나 자원이 아니라, 데이터를 얼마나 효과적으로 분석하고 활용하는지가 중요해지고 있습니다.

저희 연구실은 금융데이터사이언스, 비즈니스 애널리틱스, 데이터 기반 의사결정을 주요 연구 분야로 삼고 있습니다. 다양한 산업과 조직에서 새로운 발견(finds)을 찾아내고 의미 있는 인사이트를 발굴하고자 합니다.

✨ OUR VISION

We illuminate the future of Better Financial Intelligence ▼

We envision a future where financial intelligence diminishes knowledge asymmetry, turning complex data streams into clear, accessible, and strategically valuable insights — a future built upon meaningful finds that guide decision-makers across finance and business domains.

Research

We pursue rigorous and pioneering work.

We pursue rigorous methodologies in financial data science and business analytics, building transparent frameworks that advance knowledge while ensuring practical fidelity and data-inspired decision-making.

Impact

We transform theory into practical instruments.

We transform theory into intuitive solutions, enabling practitioners to navigate uncertainty and bridge the gap between sophisticated analytics and real-world application in both financial markets and business operations.

Philosophy

We strive toward des avenirs lucides ("lucid futures") in finance.

We strive toward the lucid future—clarity, discernment, and transparency—in finance and business, as we illuminate complexity through data science, making finance more equitable and transformative on a global scale.
